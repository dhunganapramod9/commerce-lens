22. RISKS
============================================================
Risk: Customers do not know their costs.
Mitigation:
    Start with brands that know COGS. Allow estimated cost or category-level
    margin rules later.
Risk: Customers fear price automation.
Mitigation:
    Start with reports and approval. Do not automate first.
Risk: Scraping fails.
Mitigation:
    Use customer-provided URLs, confidence scoring, manual review queue, and
    semi-manual audits early.
Risk: Reports are interesting but not paid.
Mitigation:
    Tie reports to specific dollar impact. Ask before audit: "If we find useful
    actions, would you pay for weekly monitoring?"
Risk: Too crowded.
Mitigation:
    Never pitch as price tracking. Pitch margin leak reports and pricing
    actions.
